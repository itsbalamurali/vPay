How It Works

VPay App Installation

  • Go to Google Play Store
  • Enter & Select V pay App
  • Click on Install
  • Installation Complete

Explore VPay

  • Click on Open
  • VPay App shall load & Open

Registration Page

  • Enter Mobile Number & Birth Date Registered with our Admin Portal
  • One time registration process Shall be completed
  • Login Account of the user shall be created after filling its Personal information

Mpin Page

  • After entering the Mpin selected by user, he will get access to the App and he will land to home page.

Home Page

  • After entering the Log-in credentials user will land to Home page containing multiple options for Transactions.

Aadhaar Pay

  • After clicking on Adhaar Pay Icon on home screen, this page shall open.
  • Enter Adhaar No, Select Bank & Amount.
  • Accept Terms & Conditions
  • Click on Scan & Pay

Aadhaar Pay

  • Payment Successful icon shall Appear on screen

Unified Payment Interface

  • After clicking on UPI Icon, this page shall open.
  • Enter unique VPA (generated through Banks’ UPI app), Amount, Customer Name, Bill No., Customer GST No., Remark etc.
  • Accept Terms & Conditions
  • Click on Process

UPI Collection

  • Successful Payment Collection Icon shall appear on screen

UPI QR

  • Payer shall scan this UPI QR, and shall initiate the payment.

Payment Gateway

  • After clicking on Gateway Icon, this page shall open.
  • Enter Amount
  • Accept Terms & Conditions
  • Click on Process

Payment Gateway

  • Select Payment Option
  • i.e. Net Banking / Debit / Credit Card
  • Enter Details
  • Proceed for payment
