Zero Depreciation

Every year, your two wheeler devaluates due to wear and tear of its parts. The add-on does not consider this, giving you complete coverage.


Benefits Covered (4)

Own Damage

In case of an accident, we pay the cost of damages to your two wheeler

Key Inclusions: Natural calamities (Fire, lightening, flood etc), Man-made disasters (Burglary, theft, riot, strikes etc.)

Key Exclusions: Depreciation of two wheeler parts, tyres, two wheeler driven under intoxication.

Third Party

If you accidentally ram your two wheeler into someone or something, we'll pay for the legal liabilities - injury or death of a person, and damage caused to the surrounding property.

Key Exclusions: 1. Accidents outside geographical area.

2. Two Wheeler driven by someone who is not the owner or driver.

3. Loss or damage due to war or invasion.

Two Wheeler Theft

In case your two wheeler gets stolen, we pay you the two wheeler's Insured Declared Value i.e maximum sum insured amount fixed at the beginning of the policy period.

Key Inclusions: Theft of two wheeler

Key Exclusions: Frauds, staged theft, claims made without filing an FIR

Personal Accident

We pay up to `15 lakhs in case of death or permanent disability caused to the owner-driver due to an accident.

Key Inclusions: Registered owner of the two wheeler, valid license held by the driver as per motor vehicle rules.

Key Exclusions: Accident caused outside geographical area
