General Merchant FAQ’s

What is V-pay ?

V-Pay is really a merchant's product, its feature works like a substitute for the capital-intensive point of sale machine (POS), it is also an aggregated platform for all new payment receipt channels like Aadhaar-Pay, UPI, BharatQR and UPI QR Code and Payment gateway and income receipt channels like Aadhaar enabled Micro ATM, BBPS and FASTag etc.

You can use V-pay for:
  • Accepting payments from customer through different payment modes such as Aadhaar-Pay, UPI, UPI QR Code, BharatQR Code and Payment gateway.
  • Increasing income from Income receipts channels such as Aadhaar enabled Micro ATM, BBPS, and FASTAG etc.
  • Providing Bill Payments services such as 'Mobile/ Landline/ Gas/ Electricity/ Insurance, which will generate you an extra income.
  • Money withdrawal and deposit facility from Aadhaar enabled Micro ATM given to customers, which will be an extra source of income for you.

I want to register as V-Pay merchant? What is the registration process for that ?

Registration process:
  • Register to ‘Become a V-Pay Merchant’, fill the registration form with minimum KYC available on website www.virtualpay.in and download V-Pay App from Play store and Apple IOS and fill the registration form with minimum KYC.
  • Minimum KYC details required are your Name, Mobile No. EMAIL ID, DOB, Aadhaar Number and bank account details such as bank name, Account number and IFSC Code.
  • Merchant need to upload his/her scan copy of Aadhaar card and cancelled cheque copy or scan copy of bank passbook during the registration process.
  • After registration, you will receive the V-Pay kit, which includes fingerprint scanner and user manual.
  • Get started, after successful registration; simply start accepting payments and offering various V-pay services to your customers.

What are the ways through which I can start collecting payments?

You can start collecting payments from different payment modes available on V-Pay which includes Aadhaar-Pay, UPI, UPI QR Code, Bharat QR Code and Payment Gateway.

  2. Enter amount

  5. Both customer and merchant receive notification in mobile application for successful transaction

Aadhaar Pay FAQ’s

What is Aadhaar Pay?

Aadhaar Pay is a service to allow the merchant to accept payment from a customer of any bank by authenticating the customer’s biometrics (currently only fingerprints) directly from customer’s bank account and receive the sale proceeds instantaneously into merchant’s own bank account.

How Aadhaar Pay works?

Following steps:

  • Enter customer Aadhaar number
  • Select bank name linked with Aadhaar number
  • Enter amount to pay
  • Fingerprints impression
  • Payment successful

  • V-Pay Application

  • Bank account

    UPI FAQ’s

    What is UPI?

    Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is an instant payment system. UPI is built over the IMPS infrastructure and allows you to instantly transfer money between any two parties' bank accounts.

    How UPI works?

    Following steps:

    1. Enter customer unique VPA (generated through Banks’ UPI app)
    2. Enter amount
    3. Click on pay
    4. Payment successful

    What are the requirements for this method of Payment?

    Merchants Requirements

    Merchant have to make following arrangements. These are the requirement for the person who want payment.

    • Smartphone/ PC
    • Reliable Internet connection
    • V-Pay Application (Mobile or Web)

    Customers Requirement:

    • Customer has to download his/her bank’s UPI enabled mobile banking app
    • Customer Virtual Payment Address

    What are the Benefits of UPI?

    • Seamless fund collection from customers – single identifiers
    • Customer can make a cashless transaction without swiping there credit or debit cards
    • Merchants don’t need to invest in any hardware like POS Machine for accepting digital payments
    • No need to fill bank details every time you do a transaction
    • Instant money payment
    • Available 24*7

    What is an UPI PIN?

    UPI PIN (UPI Personal Identification Number) is a 4-6 digit secret code you create/set during first time registration with this Application. You have to enter this UPI PIN to authorise all bank transactions. If you have already set up an UPI PIN with other UPI Apps you can use the same on Bharat Interface for Money.

    Do money transfers happen on UPI only during banking hours?

    All payments are instant and 24/7, regardless of your bank's working hours.

    What is the limit of fund transfer using UPI?

    At present, The transaction limits have been limited to Rs 10,000 per transaction and Rs 1,00,000 per day. However this will be reviewed and changed from time to time by RBI and depend on the banks for setting their individual limit.

    No Bank Details required

    In VPA based UPI transactions, only the receiver’s Virtual Payment Address is required to transfer funds. In other words, the receiver need not provide their bank information to the sender for transferring funds.

    UPI QR Code

    What is UPI QR Code?

    UPI QR is P2M & P2P (Person to Merchant and Person to Person) Mobile payment solution. Once the UPI QR codes are deployed on Merchant locations, user can pay the transaction amount using UPI QR enabled mobile banking apps without sharing any user credentials to the merchant. It is a quick method of payment.

    What is Static UPI QR Code?

    The static code is one wherein the merchant has the QR code on display and the customer scans it to enter the amount and make the payment.

    What is Dynamic UPI QR Code?

    The Dynamic QR code, on the other hand, can be used in places such as restaurants, where the merchant will generate a new QR code for each purchase. In this case, upon scanning the code, the amount need not be entered.

    Are Bharat QR & UPI QR Same?

    UPI Based QR preferably used for P2P or P2M dynamic Transaction using Virtual Payment Address. Bharat QR is specifically used for P2M transaction wherein payment is done via cards i.e., Debit card/Credit Card/Pre-paid Card.
